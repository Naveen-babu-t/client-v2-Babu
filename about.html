Our Story

We Are a Professional and Caring Plumbing Team

Dan Vokey's Plumbing Services was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional plumbing services while building lasting connections with our community. We believe in putting people first and working together as a team to achieve this goal.

Over the years, we've grown from a one-person operation to a trusted team of skilled plumbers serving Kingston and the surrounding areas. Through our commitment to quality workmanship and excellent customer service, we've built a reputation as one of the most reliable plumbing companies in the region.
